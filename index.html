FIRMS: Fire Incidents Reports Management System

FIRMS is a comprehensive system designed to streamline the management of fire incident reports, focusing on operational efficiency and investigative accuracy. This system offers robust capabilities tailored for fire departments and related agencies:
1. Report Management: FIRMS efficiently handles the storage, organization, and retrieval of fire incident reports, ensuring all details are securely managed and easily accessible.
2. Operation and Investigation: The system supports the management of both operational reports (incident response details, equipment used, etc.) and investigative reports (cause analysis, findings, recommendations, etc.), aiding in comprehensive incident management.
3. Exporting and Importing Reports: FIRMS facilitates seamless export and import functionalities, allowing reports to be shared across departments or with external agencies while maintaining data integrity.
4. Personnel Information Management: It includes modules for managing personnel information, such as firefighter details, training records, certifications, and contact information, ensuring efficient resource allocation and personnel management.
5. Files Management: The system allows for the secure storage and management of various files related to fire incidents, including photos, videos, witness statements, and official documents.
6. Secure Login: FIRMS features a robust authentication system with secure login credentials, ensuring authorized access to sensitive information and maintaining data confidentiality.
7. Report Trash and Retrieval: Users can manage reports effectively with features like report trash (temporary storage before permanent deletion) and report retrieval (quick access to archived or deleted reports as needed).
